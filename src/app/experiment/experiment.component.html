About us

Novodhi stands for "Innovative Intelligence" solutions for our esteemed customers.

Novodhi Solutions is a professional services company focused on next generation IT/Business digital solutions. We understand, the market space is full of IT staffing companies but there are challenges in supplying right skills with required domain and advanced IT skills. Novodhi supports organizations with their IT staff augmentation with focusing domain skills and latest technologies like RPA, BigData, Cloud, Tableau/PowerBI, AI & Machine Learning, Agile/DevOps and full stack. Novodhi Solutions is also specialized in Insurance/ Healthcare/Financial business transformations, Legacy modernization and COTS implementations. Novodhi Solutions strongly believes in solving business rather than IT problems through its staff augmentation services combined with innovative solutions. Knowledge management, Innovation and deep domain skills are three pillars of Novodhi Solutions. We proactively invest in training our staff in required/trending technologies and domain skills. Customer is central to Novodhi’s success and we bring "Novodhi" to every one of our customers.

Novodhi’s mission is to create Next Generation, Innovative digital workforce for the Industry.

Novodhi’s vision is to be a top business solutions provider in Insurance, Healthcare & Financial Services Industry through its Innovative Intelligence Solutions.
